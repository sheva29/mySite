>>> Breil Fuga is an innovative watch that beckons sophistication, it is intended as a something completely sophisticated for user ranging the ages of 30-45 years and that could top brands; that throughout time have design watch icons and still remain at the top of the market.

The default material as a requirement was stainless steel which is very elegant per se; not only combining shapes that appeal to a slick and lavish design, but a main bezel that allows customization for different occasions and moods, ranging from materials such as nickel- plated steel to ceramics. Available for both men and women, the design of the strap shows synergy among the shapes as a whole.


>>> Breil Fuga es un reloj que apela a la sofisticacion. Disenado para usuarios entre edades de 30-45 anos. Su material por requerimiento es acero inoxidable, usando formas que evocan a un diseno sofisticado y minimo. Su bisel intercambiable permite personalizacion a traves de diferentes materiales desde niquelados hasta ceramicos para distintas ocasiones.

Ademas de estar disponible para hombres y mujeres, el diseno de su pulso encuentra armonia con la base, haciendolo ver como un solo elemento.
