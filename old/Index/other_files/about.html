My conception of

Mauricio Sanchez is an industrial designer from Cali, Colombia, currently working and living in New York City. After attending ICESI University in his home town he started a career in The Big Apple, working for companies such as Glide-inc, TigerSpike, BubbleBeats, Quadrant 2 Inc., Habana Outpost, the TwistTogether company, Martel Design and Fabrication among others.
Mauricio’s design is characterized by its well-rounded sensibility, conceiving designs that are part of a system that is compelling to interact with. They are not only aesthetically appealing but practical and innovative.

Generar calidad de vida

Mauricio Sánchez es un diseñador industrial de Cali, Colombia. Actualmente vive en la ciudad de Nueva York, pronto de haber terminado sus estudios en la universidad ICESI de Cali; emigró a Estados Unidos donde ha trabajado con diferentes empresas como: Glide-inc, Tigerspike, Bubblebeats, Quadrant 2 inc, Habana outpost, The Twisttogether company, Martel Design and Fabrication entre otras.
Su diseño se caracteriza por ser sensible con su entorno, concibiendo diseños como parte de un sistema el cual es necesario y clave para la interacción; no solo a través de un diseño que apela a la estética del entorno sino siendo diferenciador al mismo tiempo.
